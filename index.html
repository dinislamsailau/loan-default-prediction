Technical Methodology

End-to-end machine learning pipeline from data preprocessing to model deployment.

Data Pipeline

  • Data Source: Lending Club accepted loans dataset (2007-2018)
  • Preprocessing: Missing value imputation, outlier handling, feature encoding
  • Feature Engineering: Created loan_to_income, debt burden ratios, risk categories
  • Class Balancing: Stratified sampling to address imbalanced dataset

Model Architecture

  • Algorithm: Random Forest Classifier (100 trees, max depth 10)
  • Baseline: Logistic Regression with class weighting
  • Validation: 80/20 train-test split with stratification
  • Metrics: ROC-AUC, Precision, Recall, F1-Score, Confusion Matrix

Market Insights & Business Impact

Actionable recommendations derived from data analysis with specific focus on financial market context.

Key Findings

  • Loan-to-Income Ratio emerged as a top-5 predictive feature, highlighting its critical importance in risk assessment.
  • Credit Purpose significantly influences default probability, with small business and debt consolidation being highest risk.
  • Regional Patterns in default rates suggest localized economic factors that warrant deeper investigation.

Recommendations

  • Enhanced Income Verification: Implement robust checks for accurate loan-to-income calculation given informal employment patterns.
  • Risk-Based Pricing: Differentiate interest rates based on loan purpose and borrower risk profile.
  • Regional Risk Models: Develop location-specific risk models accounting for economic disparities across regions.
